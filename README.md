# 📊 Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents a **1-page executive Power BI dashboard** analyzing Amazon sales performance for the years **2022–2023** using 50,000+ transaction records.

The dashboard delivers actionable insights into revenue trends, category performance, regional distribution, discount impact, and payment behavior through interactive visualizations and advanced DAX measures.

---

## 📌 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall revenue performance  
- Compare Year-over-Year (YoY) growth  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Evaluate regional revenue contribution  
- Analyze discount impact on sales  
- Understand customer payment preferences  

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- 💰 **$32.87M Total Revenue**  
- 📦 **50K Total Orders**  
- 🛒 **$657 Average Order Value (AOV)**  
- 📈 **YoY Growth %**  
- 📅 **YTD Sales Analysis**  

---

## 🔎 Deep Analytical Measures (DAX)

The dashboard includes advanced analytical calculations such as:

- `Total Revenue`
- `Total Orders (DistinctCount)`
- `Average Order Value (AOV)`
- `Sales LY (SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR)`
- `YoY Growth %`
- `YTD Sales (TOTALYTD)`
- `Revenue Without Discount`
- `Revenue Lost Due to Discounts`
- `Average Discount %`

Time intelligence functions were implemented using a custom **Date Table** to enable accurate YoY and YTD comparisons.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Monthly Revenue Trend (2022 vs 2023 comparison)  
- Revenue by Product Category  
- Revenue by Customer Region  
- Payment Method Contribution Analysis  
- Discount vs Revenue Scatter Analysis  
- Interactive Slicers (Year, Region, Category)  
- Executive KPI Panel for quick performance tracking  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Beauty and Books emerged as top-performing categories (~$5.5M+ each).  
- Revenue distribution across regions is balanced, with Middle East and North America slightly leading.  
- Discount levels (~13%) show moderate impact on revenue.  
- Wallet and UPI represent a significant share of transactions.
